Black Rhino CE
Browse the Internet and Earn Zcash
How it Works?
Black Rhino CE is a treasure hunt based Chrome Extension that allows users to earn Zcash by visiting advertised websites on the platform.
The amount of Zcash earned is based off of a tier system where you can earn more if you find advertised websites faster than other users
Creating an Account
To create a Black Rhino CE account, enter your email address and the country you are residing in.
Verify your account by clicking the verification link sent by 'info@blackrhino-ce.com'. This is the only address you will recieve any Black Rhino CE related information from.
